About the Convention

Kunterbunt is a FLINTA-centered juggling convention celebrating color, creativity, and community. We focus on safer spaces, accessible workshops, and joyful movement.

Inclusive Workshops

Skill tracks in juggling, object manipulation, clowning, and flow arts with multilingual instruction.

Well-being & Care

Dedicated quiet rooms, peer support teams, and accessibility stewards to make the weekend comfortable.

Community Nights

Open stages, jam circles, and community dinners with vegetarian and vegan options.

FAQ

Need to know more? We have you covered.

Who can attend?

The convention is for FLINTA participants and allies who respect the safer space guidelines.

Where is it held?

Kulturcampus Berlin, with indoor studios, outdoor lawns, and on-site camping.

Is there financial support?

Yes. We reserve discounted tickets and travel grants. Email hello@kunterbunt-juggling.org.
