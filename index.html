Kunterbunt Juggling Festival 2025

FLINTA+ is an inclusive umbrella term used for women, lesbians, intersex, non-binary, trans, and agender people. Kunterbunt Juggling Festival is built as a safer and joyful space where this community and allies can practice, learn, and celebrate together. During three vibrant days, participants share tricks in the open hall, join workshops, collaborate in passing sessions, and enjoy shows designed around solidarity and creativity.
